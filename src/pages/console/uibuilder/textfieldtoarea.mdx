  description: `How to convert existing TextField primitive components to TextAreaField primitive components`

After [upgrading](/console/uibuilder/figmatocode/#upgrading-figma-files) to the current Figma community file, you can change any existing TextField into a TextAreaField by selecting the desired TextField instance and choosing "TextAreaField" under the Swap Instance dropdown.
