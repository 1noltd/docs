  description: `How to convert existing TextField primitive components to TextAreaField primitive components`

After [upgrading](/console/uibuilder/figmatocode/#upgrading-figma-files) to the current Figma community file. You can update any existing TextFields into TextAreaFields by selecting the desired component and changing the instance of the TextField component to the TextAreaField component.
